# 3-Day Travel Itinerary in Paris for Music Lovers

## Overview
Welcome to Paris, the City of Lights! This 3-day itinerary is designed to immerse you in the city's rich musical heritage and festive holiday atmosphere while ensuring you experience its iconic sights and culinary delights.

### Travel Dates: 24 December - 26 December 2023

### Budget Breakdown (per person)
- **Flights**: $75 round-trip from London
- **Accommodation**: $100 per night at Hotel Le Grey (2 nights = $200)
- **Food**: $50 per day (3 days = $150)
- **Activities**: $150 (concerts, attractions)
- **Transportation**: $50 (Metro pass)
  
**Total Estimated Budget**: $625

---

## Day 1: Arrival and City Highlights

### Morning
- **Arrival**: Fly from London to Paris in the morning. 
- **Accommodation Check-in**: Hotel Le Grey, a charming hotel located in the 9th arrondissement, perfect for music enthusiasts and centrally located. 

### Afternoon
- **Lunch at Café de Flore**: Enjoy a classic Parisian meal in this iconic café known for its historical significance among artists and musicians.
- **Visit the Eiffel Tower**: Take an elevator ride to the second floor for breathtaking city views, beautifully adorned for the holidays.

### Evening
- **Dinner at Le Relais de l’Entrecôte**: Famous for its steak-frites and secret sauce, perfect for a hearty meal.
- **Christmas Concert at the American Cathedral**: Experience a delightful evening of classical music in a beautiful Gothic revival church.

### Weather
- **Conditions**: Cloudy with light drizzle
- **Packing Suggestions**: Warm layers, waterproof jacket, and comfortable walking shoes.

---

## Day 2: Music and Culture

### Morning
- **Breakfast at the hotel**: Enjoy a French breakfast of croissants and coffee.
- **Visit the Louvre**: Explore the world-renowned art collections that make the museum a must-see.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch at Le Soufflé**: Delight in traditional French soufflés. 
- **Explore Montmartre**: Wander through the charming streets, visiting the Sacré-Cœur Basilica for stunning views.

### Evening
- **Dinner at Bouillon Pigalle**: Enjoy affordable classic French dishes in a lively atmosphere.
- **Watch 'The Nutcracker' at Opéra Bastille**: A beautiful ballet performance perfect for the holiday season.

### Weather
- **Conditions**: Chance of rain, stay warm!
- **Packing Suggestions**: Layered clothing, umbrella, and stylish evening attire for the opera.

---

## Day 3: Markets and Departure

### Morning
- **Breakfast at Angelina**: Famous for its hot chocolate and pastries.
- **Visit Christmas Markets at Champs Élysées**: Enjoy shopping for unique gifts and savor seasonal treats.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch at Le Pain Quotidien**: Casual dining with organic selections.
- **Hôtel de Ville Ice Skating**: Enjoy skating in front of City Hall, a magical experience during winter.

### Evening
- **Farewell Dinner at La Coupole**: Celebrate your last night with seafood and Parisian classics in a historic brasserie. 
- **Metro to the airport**: Head to Charles de Gaulle Airport for your flight back to London.

### Weather
- **Conditions**: Cool, no expected precipitation
- **Packing Suggestions**: Winter coat, scarf, and gloves for an enjoyable outing.

---

## Packing List
- Warm clothing (layers)
- Waterproof jacket and footwear
- Gloves, scarf, and hat
- Formal attire for the opera
- Chargers for electronic devices
- Travel-sized toiletries

Enjoy your enchanting Parisian holiday, filled with music, culture, and delightful culinary experiences!
