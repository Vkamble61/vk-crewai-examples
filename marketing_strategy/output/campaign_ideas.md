This platform will allow participants to ask questions and engage in real-time discussions about overcoming barriers to entry.  
**Expected Impact:** Positioning CrewAI as a thought leader in the space, driving engagement while showcasing CrewAI expertise, ultimately converting attendees into leads over time.

**Expected Impact:** Increased follower engagement and brand visibility on social media platforms, attracting more enterprise clients interested in CrewAI’s solutions and ultimately leading to conversion.
