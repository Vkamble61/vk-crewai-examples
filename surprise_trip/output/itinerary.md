# Comprehensive 5-Day Itinerary for London, December 2025

## **Day 1: Arrival in London**

- **Flight Information:**
  - Departure: Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)
  - Arrival: London Heathrow Airport (LHR)
  - Date: December 3, 2025
  - Airline: Virgin Atlantic
  - Flight Duration: Approximately 10 hours
  - Price: $581 round trip

- **Hotel Information:**
  - Accommodation: The Bloomsbury Hotel, London
  - Address: 16-22 Great Russell St, Bloomsbury, London WC1B 3NN, UK
  - Description: A stylish and chic hotel located in the heart of London, perfect for both leisure and business travelers.
  - Check-in: December 3, 2025
  - Check-out: December 7, 2025

- **Restaurant**: Agora
  - **Location**: 2-4 Bedale Street, Borough, London SE1 9AL
  - **Cuisine**: Greek-inspired
  - **Description**: A buzzy, boisterous souvla bar with a vibrant atmosphere and delicious Greek-inspired dishes.
  - **Rating**: Highly recommended for its lively environment and authentic flavors.

- **Activity**: See the Christmas Lights on Regent & Oxford Street
  - **Location**: Regent Street, London
  - **Description**: Experience the iconic Christmas lights from an open-top bus tour, a unique way to see the city's festive decorations.


## **Day 2: Explore London**

- **Restaurant**: Barrafina Dean Street
  - **Location**: 26-27 Dean Street, Soho, London W1D 3LL
  - **Cuisine**: Spanish Tapas
  - **Description**: Enjoy authentic tapas in a lively, reservation-free environment, with dishes prepared right in front of you.
  - **Rating**: Known for its vibrant atmosphere and top-notch tapas.

- **Activity**: Visit a Christmas Market
  - **Location**: Christmas by the River, London
  - **Description**: Explore the market set alongside the River Thames, offering a variety of foods, crafts, and festive drinks.


## **Day 3: Cultural Immersion**

- **Restaurant**: Josephine Chelsea
  - **Location**: 315 Fulham Road, Chelsea, London SW10 9QH
  - **Cuisine**: French Bistro
  - **Description**: A classic French bistro offering traditional Lyonnaise specialties in a bustling atmosphere.
  - **Rating**: Praised for its classic dishes and charming ambiance.

- **Activity**: See the Light Show at Kew Gardens
  - **Location**: Kew Gardens, Richmond, London
  - **Description**: Walk through a beautiful light display in the gardens, a magical experience during the festive season.


## **Day 4: Adventure and Relaxation**

- **Restaurant**: Kiln
  - **Location**: 58 Brewer Street, Soho, London W1F 9TL
  - **Cuisine**: Thai
  - **Description**: A homage to Northern Thai cuisine with a menu inspired by seasonal British produce.
  - **Rating**: Celebrated for its intense flavors and unique BBQ experience.

- **Activity**: Ice Skating at Somerset House
  - **Location**: Somerset House, London
  - **Description**: Enjoy a classic winter activity in one of London's most beautiful settings.


## **Day 5: Departure**

- **Restaurant**: Masala Zone Piccadilly Circus
  - **Location**: 224 Piccadilly, St. James's, London W1J 9HP
  - **Cuisine**: Indian
  - **Description**: Affordable Indian favorites in a grand and glittering setting, perfect for a final meal in London.
  - **Rating**: Known for its vibrant dishes and stunning interior.

- **Activity**: Stroll Through Covent Garden
  - **Location**: Covent Garden, London
  - **Description**: Explore the festive decorations, pop-up shops, and the famous Christmas tree, capturing the essence of the holiday season.

  - Departure: London Heathrow Airport (LHR)
